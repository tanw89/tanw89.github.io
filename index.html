A little about me

Hello there!


My name is Weilie and this is my personal portfolio site.

A tinkerer by nature, I often foray into the world of art, music and code in my free time. I hope this little space can give you a glimpse into my thoughts. These creations are far from perfect, but each piece of work is a small effort to leave the world (both digital and offline) slightly better.

By day, I work in a government service design lab. I am fortunate to be able to exercise my interests in data science, service design, UX and public policy in my job, where I create products and services for public good. If your interests overlap with mine, or if you are interested in what I do, ping me for coffee or tea!
