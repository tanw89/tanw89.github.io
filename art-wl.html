Sketchy Details

How Art Thou

I first started sketching on a more regular basis in 2013. Sketching allowed me to take in sights more intentionally, and to truly appreciate the moment instead of clamouring to capture it on camera, which sometimes “dilutes” the experience and memory.

I hope to be able to sketch better, and when time permits, try out other media such as acrylic or watercolour. It's just hard, because time keeps slipping away, like in that Salvador Dali painting. Hmm.

Inequality, A Diptych
2017

×


As a sociology student, I have always been very intrigued by inequality. This diptych was birthed out of that interest.

The idea for this came about when I stepped out of Bugis Plus one evening in 2017 to see a large rainbow. I was aware of the sparkling, glitzy mall behind me, MBS in the background, and the hawker centre in front of me where some elderly men were chatting over beer.

I couldn't help but wonder how each person saw the same rainbow.
