Listen to my music

When words fail...

Music speaks. Well it's been speaking to me since I was a child, and I hope to speak through it to express things I may not otherwise be able to through conversation alone. Being clasically trained, I am interested in repertoire from the Baroque to the 20th century. I have a particular liking for sacred music by Bach and Handel, and clean tonal sonatas by Scarlatti. I also quite like Nirvana and Deep Purple -- such a strange mix.

I have recently tried to record more jazz renderings of standards or oldies, and looking to improve my jazz harmony and theory.
